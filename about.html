About Data Insights

Data Insights is a transparent data collection platform designed to help businesses understand their users better through ethical data practices.

Our Mission

To provide valuable user insights while maintaining the highest standards of privacy, transparency, and user consent.

Our Commitment

  • ✓ Transparency: We clearly disclose what data we collect and how we use it
  • ✓ Consent: We only collect data from users who explicitly agree
  • ✓ Security: Your data is protected with industry-standard encryption
  • ✓ Control: You have full control over your data and can request deletion anytime
  • ✓ Privacy: We never sell your data to third parties

How It Works

  1. Users voluntarily provide basic information (name, email, age)
  2. Our tracking system records behavioral and technical data
  3. Data is securely stored and analyzed for insights
  4. Results help improve our service and user experience

Data Collection Methods

We use JavaScript-based tracking (stored in localStorage and sent to our server) to collect:

  • User interactions (clicks, page views, time spent)
  • Browser and device information
  • Approximate location data
  • Session behavior and patterns

Legal Compliance

Data Insights is designed to comply with major privacy regulations including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act)
