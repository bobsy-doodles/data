Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Data Insights ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Basic Information (You Provide)

  • Name: Full name for identification
  • Email Address: For communication and identification
  • Age: To understand demographic insights

2.2 Behavioral Data (Automatically Collected)

  • Page Views: Which pages you visit and when
  • Time Spent: How long you spend on each page
  • Clicks: What elements you interact with
  • Scroll Depth: How far down pages you scroll
  • Session Duration: Total time on our website

2.3 Technical Data (Automatically Collected)

  • Browser Information: Type and version (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
  • Device Type: Desktop, tablet, or mobile
  • Operating System: Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, etc.
  • IP Address: For general location and network analysis
  • Location: Approximate location based on IP (city/region level)
  • Screen Resolution: For UI/UX optimization

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1 Analytics & Insights

  • Understanding how users interact with our website
  • Identifying popular features and content
  • Measuring user engagement and satisfaction

3.2 Product Improvement

  • Optimizing website performance and user experience
  • Identifying and fixing bugs or issues
  • Developing new features based on user behavior
  • A/B testing improvements

3.3 Marketing

  • Understanding user demographics and interests
  • Creating targeted content and recommendations
  • Measuring campaign effectiveness
  • Sending newsletters and updates (with your consent)

3.4 Legal & Safety

  • Protecting against fraud or abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and protected with industry-standard encryption and security measures. We do not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to provide our services and for legal compliance purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of behavioral tracking by disabling cookies

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect behavioral and technical data. You can control cookies through your browser settings or opt-out by contacting us.
